Rock Paper Scissors

How to Play

The rules are simple! Select your move to challenge the computer. Remember: Rock crushes Scissors, Paper covers Rock, and Scissors cuts Paper.

Controls

Mouse: Hover over an image and click to make your choice.

Keyboard: Press R for Rock, P for Paper, or S for Scissors.

If you want to start over at any time, just click the Play Again! button to reset the game.
